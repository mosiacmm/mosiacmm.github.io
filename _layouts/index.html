Research

We explore issues of identity, statelessness, and unofficial minorities to promote inclusion, equal citizenship, and evidence-based advocacy in Myanmar.

Education

We advance inclusive learning that builds awareness of diversity, challenges discrimination, and strengthens community understanding and belonging.

Dialogue

We lead dialogues on citizenship, minority management, transitional justice, social integration, and the role of religion to promote inclusion in a federal democracy.

Our Guiding Principles

Mosaic Myanmar believes that all people, regardless of race, religion, and gender, are empowered and have the right to participate fully in their societies and to lead dignified social and economic lives.

About our Organisation

Better together better Myanmar

Mosaic Myanmar is an independent national non-governmental organization based in Chiang Mai/Thailand and Yangon/Myanmar.

The organization was founded on November 25, 2016.
